Why Choose Us!

Our Specialization And Company Features

At Taheri General Traders, we are committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience when shopping for hardware and building supplies. Here are just a few reasons why you should choose us:

Quality Products:
We source our products from only the best and most reputable manufacturers to ensure that our customers receive high-quality, durable products that stand the test of time.
Competitive Prices:
We understand that every penny counts, which is why we offer competitive pricing on all of our products and services. You won't find better value for your money anywhere else.
Exceptional Customer Service:
We are dedicated to providing our customers with exceptional customer service, from the moment you walk through our doors to long after your purchase is made. We strive to make every interaction with our customers a positive one.
Local Business:
We are a proud local business and have been serving the Mariakani, Kilifi county community for many years. When you choose Taheri General Traders, you are supporting a local business and helping to strengthen the community.
